Donations can be made by credit card at: http://support.ubc.ca/tarbotton-scholarship
Canadian residents will receive a tax receipt for the full value of their donation.

International donors who wish to receive a tax benefit have the option of donating by cheque to one of UBC's partner foundations based in the United States, the United Kindgom or Hong Kong. Unfortunately it is not possible to donate by credit card to these foundations. Please see below for more information.

United States

To donate and receive an American tax receipt, donations can be made to The American Foundation for The University of British Columbia , a registered charity under US law (charitable registration number 52-1559117). A donation receipt will be issued to American donors by the Foundation for the full amount of the gift in US dollars.

Please make your gift payable by cheque (from a US bank only) to The American Foundation for The University of British Columbia and mark on the cheque memo field: Becky Tarbotton Memorial Scholarship.

Mailing Address:

The American Foundation for The University of British Columbia
#155 - 1718 M Street N.W.
Washington, D.C. 20036
IRS #521559117

United Kingdom

The UK Foundation of The University of British Columbia is a registered charity with the Charity Commission (charity registration number 1103437). UK supporters are encouraged to complete a Gift Aid Form (PDF) to ensure the maximum tax treatment of their gift.

Please make your gift payable by cheque to The UK Foundation of The University of British Columbia and mark on the cheque memo field: Becky Tarbotton Memorial Scholarship.

Hong Kong

The Hong Kong Foundation for UBC Limited is recognized as a charity under the Inland Revenue Ordinance of Hong Kong (business number 444488). All donations to the Foundation will be eligible for tax benefits similar to those received when giving to any other educational or charitable organization in Hong Kong.

Please make your gift payable by cheque to The Hong Kong Foundation for UBC Limited and mark on the cheque memo field: Becky Tarbotton Memorial Scholarship.
