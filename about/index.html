The Scholarship

The Becky Tarbotton Memorial Scholarship supports a second year graduate students in the School of Community and Regional Planning in the Faculty of Applied Science who demonstrate exceptional leadership and commitment in the fields of environmental and social justice. The scholarship has been made available by the Tarbotton family and friends in memory of Rebecca (“Becky”) Tarbotton. Becky was a leader and visionary who graduated from SCARP and dedicated her career to protecting the environment and defending
